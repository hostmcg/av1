The Blind Spot

Retail has a trillion-dollar visibility problem.

Legacy systems detect problems after the damage is done. The gap between what's supposed to be on the shelf and what's actually there costs the industry more than most companies' entire revenue.

Snapshot Data, Not Reality

Periodic audits and manual shelf checks capture moments in time — missing the majority of out-of-stock events that happen between walks.

Detection Without Action

Most systems stop at "there's a problem." They generate alerts and dashboards but don't orchestrate the fix — leaving store teams to figure it out alone.

Systems That Don't See

Inventory records say one thing. The shelf says another. Phantom inventory, mis-shelved products, and backroom disconnects create a gap no forecast can bridge.

Expensive to Scale

Existing shelf-monitoring infrastructure requires massive capital outlay — putting real-time visibility out of reach for mid-market and regional retailers.

About

We're building the connective tissue between physical retail and AI.

Retail in 2025 is no longer online vs. offline — it's one continuous, connected journey. Yet the physical store remains a black box. Inventory systems are disconnected from shelf reality. Operations teams fly blind. And customers walk away.

Aisleview was founded on a simple premise: the next wave of retail winners won't be the brands with the most channels, but the ones that can orchestrate data, stores, and operations as one system.

We're a team of spatial computing, computer vision, and retail operations veterans building the platform that makes every interaction in a store visible, actionable, and intentional.

🎯 Zero Disruption

We fit into your operations, not the other way around. No workflows change. No retraining. Just intelligence layered on top.

🔒 Stealthy By Design

Our technology is ambient and invisible. Customers experience better-stocked shelves — without ever knowing how.

⚡ Action, Not Alerts
